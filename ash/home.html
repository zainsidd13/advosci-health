OUR MISSION
AdvoSci Health (ASH) is a non-profit, student-led organization that seeks to advocate for those affected by rare medical conditions. Our goal is to address the challenges faced by individuals and families who are affected by diseases that are typically undermined due to a lack of awareness, resources, and treatments. We seek to convey information about rare conditions with hopes to inspire others to advocate and act for the benefit of communities everywhere.
OUR SIMPLE MODEL

SHARE

Share information regarding rare medical conditions and their respective treatments/diagnosis

IMPLEMENT

Implement strategies that address issues faced by persons with targeted conditions

MOTIVATE

Motivate participants to spread awareness on the chosen illness

PREDICT

Predict future outlooks of rare medical conditions

LEAD

Lead communities with professional conduct that respects the rights of others from an ethical perspective

EMPATHIZE

Empathize with individuals who live with rare medical conditions
